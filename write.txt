Introduction
Web development involves the creation and maintenance of websites and web applications that are accessible via the internet. It encompasses a broad range of tasks, from coding and technical design to ensuring the performance and functionality of a website or application.
By “website,” we mean a collection of web pages that are publicly accessible on the internet, such as:
•	School website
•	Google.com
•	Netflix.com etc.
